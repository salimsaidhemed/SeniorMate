# One workspace for connected care

<p class="section-intro">SeniorMate brings the records and daily workflows of a care organization together without losing the clarity each role needs.</p>

<div class="capability-grid">
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Care coordination</span>
    <h2>Patient management</h2>
    <p>Maintain demographics, emergency contacts, status, diagnosis summaries, profile photos, and a unified patient history.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Operations</span>
    <h2>Visits</h2>
    <p>Schedule and record caregiver or nursing visits with staff, timing, status, notes, and linked documentation.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Clinical documentation</span>
    <h2>Care notes</h2>
    <p>Capture structured Home Health Aide Notes and Nurses Progress Notes through focused, readable forms.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Clinical review</span>
    <h2>Assessments</h2>
    <p>Record fall-risk, nutrition, mobility, cognitive, and general assessments, with optional visit linkage.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Private storage</span>
    <h2>Medical records</h2>
    <p>Upload and manage patient documents in private MinIO storage while PostgreSQL retains searchable metadata.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Insight</span>
    <h2>Reports and analytics</h2>
    <p>Explore patient census, visit, staff, assessment, and medical-record activity with filters and CSV exports.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Identity</span>
    <h2>Authentication and RBAC</h2>
    <p>Use Keycloak OIDC login and backend-enforced roles for administrators, managers, nurses, caregivers, and viewers.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Administration</span>
    <h2>Users and branding</h2>
    <p>Manage Keycloak users and adapt organization names, logos, colors, banner text, and footer content.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="capability">
    <span class="eyebrow">Communication</span>
    <h2>Printable summaries</h2>
    <p>Review or print patient, visit, note, and assessment reports using clear browser-friendly layouts.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Built for evaluation

SeniorMate includes explicitly gated fictional demo data for local evaluation. It can populate patients, visits, notes, assessments, records, dashboard activity, and reports without using real patient information.
