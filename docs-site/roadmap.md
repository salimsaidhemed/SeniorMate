# Roadmap

<p class="section-intro">SeniorMate v1.0.0 establishes the core platform. The next milestones focus on accountability, communication, scheduling, organizational scale, and deeper insight.</p>

<div class="roadmap-grid">
  <section class="roadmap-panel">
    <h2>Completed</h2>
    <p><strong>v1.0.0 Initial Public Release</strong></p>
    <ul>
      <li>Core patient and visit workflows</li>
      <li>Clinical notes and assessments</li>
      <li>Medical records and patient photos</li>
      <li>Reporting, printing, and demo data</li>
      <li>Authentication, RBAC, branding, and users</li>
      <li>Documentation and public project website</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
  <section class="roadmap-panel">
    <h2>Upcoming</h2>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Audit Logging</strong> for attributable business and clinical activity.</li>
      <li><strong>Notifications</strong> with preferences and delivery tracking.</li>
      <li><strong>Scheduling</strong> for recurring visits, assignments, and conflicts.</li>
      <li><strong>Multi-tenant Organizations</strong> with scoped data and settings.</li>
      <li><strong>Advanced Analytics</strong> for richer operational trends.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
</div>

The roadmap may evolve as real deployments and user feedback clarify priorities.
