## Connected detail workflows

Patient Detail brings visits, Medical Records, Assessments, Notes, photos, and printable summaries into one patient-centered view. Aide Note and Nurse Note screens use grouped sections so long clinical forms remain readable in create, edit, detail, and print modes.
