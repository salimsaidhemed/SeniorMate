# Getting Started

SeniorMate helps home-care teams manage patients, visits, care notes,
assessments, medical records, reports, and printable summaries in one place.

## Sign In

1. Open the SeniorMate URL provided by your administrator.
2. Select **Sign In** when prompted.
3. Complete the Keycloak login form.
4. After authentication, SeniorMate opens the dashboard.

Your visible navigation and actions depend on your assigned role. Contact an
administrator if a required screen or action is missing.

## Main Navigation

- **Dashboard** shows current patient and visit activity.
- **Patients** contains demographics, contacts, photos, records, assessments,
  visits, and printable summaries.
- **Visits** tracks scheduled and completed care activity.
- **Aide Notes** and **Nurse Notes** contain role-specific documentation.
- **Reports** provides operational summaries and CSV export.
- **Settings** and **Admin** appear only for authorized roles.

## First Workflow

1. Open **Patients** and find the patient.
2. Review the patient overview and recent activity.
3. Open or create a visit if your role permits it.
4. Complete the appropriate aide note, nurse note, or assessment.
5. Use a print action when a browser-ready summary is needed.

## Data Safety

- Use only organization-approved devices and accounts.
- Do not share exported files outside approved channels.
- Confirm that you selected the correct patient before adding clinical data.
- Sign out when using a shared workstation.

See [Roles and Permissions](roles-and-permissions.md) for current access rules.
