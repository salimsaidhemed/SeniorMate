# Nurse Notes

Nurse Notes capture clinical progress documentation for a patient visit. Each
visit can have at most one Nurse Note.

## Clinical Sections

The form groups information into:

- Patient and visit information
- Vital signs and pain
- Sensory and neurological assessment
- Respiratory, cardiac, and circulation
- Genitourinary, gastrointestinal, and endocrine
- Skin integrity and wound evaluation
- Mental, functional, and homebound status
- Skilled nursing and response to intervention
- MD contact, discharge planning, feedback, and narrative
- Signature

## Create, View, and Edit

Open a visit and choose the Nurse Note action. The patient and visit are
preselected. The first version intentionally keeps most clinical sections
flexible and stores structured sections as JSON.

If a note already exists, use the view or edit action rather than creating a
duplicate.

Nurses, managers, and administrators can manage Nurse Notes. Caregivers and
viewers have read access under the current role policy.
