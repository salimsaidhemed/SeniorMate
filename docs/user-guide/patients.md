# Patient Management

The Patients area is the primary starting point for clinical and operational
work.

![SeniorMate patient list with filters](../images/patients.jpg)

## Find a Patient

Use the search field to match name, phone, email, or diagnosis summary. Status
and gender filters can narrow the results. Pagination controls move through
larger result sets.

## Create or Edit

Authorized users can create and edit patient records. First and last name are
required. Review contact details, emergency contact, diagnosis summary, and
status before saving.

## Patient Detail

The detail page brings together:

- Demographics and emergency contact
- Patient photo or initials fallback
- Visits and care-note activity
- Medical records
- Assessments
- Printable patient summary

## Delete

Deletion requires confirmation and may remove linked domain records. Use it
only when the record should be permanently removed under your organization's
policy. Inactivation is usually preferable when historical data must remain.
